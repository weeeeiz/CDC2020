What I Want to Say

Wildfires in history have resulted in casualities, emissions of toxic pollutants, increased possibilities of other natural disasters like floods, and many more consequences. Especially in this special time of covid, wildfires caused more people to have respiratory problems, which increased the demand for medical supples. I hope not only Californians, but all human race can pay attention to this issue and start protecting the air quality right away.
